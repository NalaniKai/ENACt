Puu Kukui Elementary vs. Lihikai Elementary Recycling Drive

Tags: recycling, Philippines, waste reduction

Puu Kukui Elementary School vs. Lihikai Elementary School
Challenge: Can or Bottle Drive. Collect as many recyclable cans or bottles within a given month. The school that collects the most over the period of 4 school weeks wins. All proceeds raised from the recycling fees will be donated to a fund that was established for the foundation of a recycling center in a semi-rural community in the Philippines.

University of Rotorua Carpool Challenge: Teachers vs. Physical Plant

Tags: transportation, carpool

University of Rotorua: Teachers vs. Physical Plant Car Challenge
Challenge: All teachers (30 persons) and physical plant workers (30 persons) at the University of Rotorua currently drive personal vehicles to work. The challenge is to see which group can reduce their total car count the most over a three week period. Every morning a selected individual will tally the total number of cars belonging to individuals of either teachers or physical plant workers. The group with the lowest car count of three work weeks will win free bus passes for use in the city.
